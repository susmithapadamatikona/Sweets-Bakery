Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery and our sweet creations.

Do you offer same-day delivery?+

Yes! Orders placed before 12 PM are eligible for same-day delivery within city limits. Custom cakes require at least 24–48 hours notice.

Can I customise a cake for a special occasion?+

Absolutely. Our cake artists love a challenge — share your theme, flavour and reference images through our contact form or call us, and we'll craft something unique.

Are your products suitable for people with allergies?+

We offer eggless, gluten-free and nut-free options for many products. Please note our kitchen handles nuts, dairy and gluten, so we cannot guarantee zero cross-contamination.

How should I store my cake once delivered?+

Cream cakes should be refrigerated and consumed within 2–3 days. Breads and cookies stay fresh at room temperature in an airtight container for up to a week.

Do you cater for large events and weddings?+

Yes, we specialise in weddings, corporate events and parties. Contact us at least two weeks in advance for large orders so we can plan every delicious detail.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking and cash on delivery. All online payments are securely encrypted.

Can I track my order?+

Once your order is confirmed you'll receive updates via email and SMS. Registered users can also track order status from their profile dashboard.

Do you use preservatives?+

Never. Everything is baked fresh daily using natural, premium ingredients with absolutely no artificial preservatives.

Still have questions? We're happy to help.
